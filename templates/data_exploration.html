Wine Enthusiast Data Exploration

Wine Data Sample (1000 rows)

The dataset was scraped from the Wine Enthusiast website on June 2017. The data contains reviews for a variety of wines worldwide. The wines were graded on a scale of 80-100pts by wine enthusiast reviewers. The reviewers did not know the price of each wine tested during the review. Apart from the points and price per wine, the data includes the reviewer's description of the wine tasted, wine variety, the country, province, region and even winery where the wine was produced. During our exploration, we decided to add a classifier column describing the color of wine and classified wines by price catagory.
